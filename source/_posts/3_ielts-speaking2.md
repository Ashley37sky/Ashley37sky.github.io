# hot-pot

+ is a chinese food
+ While a hot pot full of soup is kept boiling, raw ingredients are placed into the pot 
+  Most raw foods can be cooked in a hot pot, although they may have different cooking times
+ The cooked food is often eaten with a dipping [sauce](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sauce) for additional flavoring.
+ Hot pots may be prepared and eaten either at home or in a restaurant. Since it is considered as a main dish, it can be enjoyed without other separate courses like rice or noodles. 

## how to eat

+ Choose the soup base 
+ Make your own dipping sauce
+ cook food in a hot pot and eat. The best order of eating hot pot is to eat meat first, then vegetables

## why popular

+ convenient

+ enhance friendship and unite family members or colleagues as several people sit around a pot, talking and eating
+  The warm atmosphere makes people feel comfortable and relaxed.[[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hot_pot#cite_note-2) Hot pot is especially popular in winter and cold places.
